Code of Conduct

1. Do not share user information

  • Do not share the information be it personal or web related without the explicit consent from said person.
  • Only share information that is already in the public domain
  • Any sharing of personal information, such as another persons home address, will end with the termination of your account.

2. Adult Content

  • Sexually explicit material, pornography, is not to be posted or linked to on this site.
  • Artistic content may be posted/linked to however it would be advised to check with a Administrator.

3. Use of AI content

  • Any use of AI content must be made known in any post containing AI content.
  • If AI content is not labeled it could be removed or edited by an Administrator to Label the AI content being used

4. Be Respectful

  • Never publish material that is obscene, racist, sexist, pornographic, sexually exploitative, or in any other way discriminatory, threatening or harassing, personally offensive, defamatory, or illegal.
  • Infringing on this will get your account removed.

5. Use of Copyright material

  • You may link to Copyright material, however to avoid issues you will have to disclose the Copyright of the linked material
  • Do not upload Copyrighted material to the site without the express permission from the Copyright holder

6. Personal Responsibility

  • By joining This Digital Garden you are responsible for what you post.
  • Any breaches of the above rules may lead to your account being locked out either Temporarily or Permanently
  • In most instances we wont need to take action in locking user accounts, this is not a dictatorship. However depending on the severity and past history this may be the result.
